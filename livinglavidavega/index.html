Our Story

Josh and Vanessa met and became good friends through a mutual friend, Luke, Josh’s best man. One day at a friend’s party, mid conversation, without permission, Josh leaned in and kissed Vanessa...but Vanessa didn’t kiss back! The next words out of her mouth would ring in Josh’s head for days and days, “Ummm, what are you doing?”, she said. Josh abruptly turned around, walked away, and immediately found Luke. Josh asked, “I thought you said Vanessa liked me?!” Luke's mischievous laughter and joy in Josh's confusion made it apparent that Luke had played a prank on him.

Three days later, Josh finally decided to respond to Vanessa’s constant messages. She wasn’t angry with him, but only liked him as a friend, nothing else. They eventually talked about what happened and something strange happened as Josh and Vanessa stayed friends even while Josh was in Tallahassee studying at FSU... like something straight out of a movie, Vanessa started to fall for Josh! Quite embarrassed, but fueled by her girlfriends' encouragement and love of potential heart-break dramas, Vanessa met up with Josh one weekend at homecoming in Gainesville in the hopes that Josh felt the same about her, and well, I think you know how that ended.
